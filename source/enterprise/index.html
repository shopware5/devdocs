Enterprise Client Administration

The Enterprise client administration is a management tool for multiple Shopware instances. With this tool you are able to provide central backend logins for all your users to all your Shopware shops. Furthermore it will synchronize your Shopware licenses and update, install and clone shops.

The Enterprise client administration can be downloaded from the Shopware account, if an enterprise license with subscription is available.
